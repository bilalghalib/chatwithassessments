Question: What is the motivation behind your entrepreneurial pursuits?
Answer: I grew up and lives in the Jabal Mohsen area of Tripoli. While the state of the environment in Lebanon is critical, waste pervades the streets of the Bab al Tabbaneh and Jabal Mohsen area. This was only exacerbated during the 2015 garbage crisis. I saw that the traditional Solid Waste Management (SWM) systems in place were not designed to be sustainable and the contract that the main waste firms in Lebanon had with the government did not incentivise any kind of recycling. This was especially visible in the way that the government simply heaped garbage onto overburdened landfills around the country. At the same time, linked to the former, I realised that a mentality shift needs to occur with the Lebanese waste culture. Thus new business models, where the materials are properly sorted and processed, with the end effect of giving them new value, together with a shifting of local waste mentalities, had to be developed.
